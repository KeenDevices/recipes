Ingredients:
 -Peanut Butter
 -Jelly
 -Bread

 Directions:
 1. Lay two pieces of bread on a plate
 2. Spread jelly on one piece
 3. Spread peanut butter on the other piece
 4. Put bread together so the peanut butter and jelly face each other
 5. Enjoy!

 Optional:
 1. Get you a glass of milk to drink while eating your pbj
